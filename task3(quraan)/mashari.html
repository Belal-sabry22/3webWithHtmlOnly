Mishari bin Rashed Alafasy is a Kuwaiti qāriʾ, imam, preacher, and nasheed artist.
He studied in the Islamic University of Madinah's College of Qur'an, specializing in the ten qira'at and tafsir.
Born: September 5, 1976 · Kuwait City, Kuwait
Occupation(s):Qari, imam
Genres: Islamic, Nasheed, Hamd, Na'at, Qirat
Instrument(s): Vocals
Years active: 1997–present
